Questions

Everything you need to know.

When do I pay? ▾
Nothing is due today. This is a pre-order — you submit your graduate's info and reserve your spot. Payment is collected when your order is ready to ship.
How personalized is it really? ▾
Very. Every book is built from your intake form — your graduate's actual favorite memory, their real challenge, their specific future goal. We review every output before print to make sure it couldn't have been written for anyone else.
What grades does this work for? ▾
Pre-K through college graduation. The storybook writing level adjusts automatically based on the grade — a kindergarten book reads differently than a 12th grade one. The comic and figurine work for any age.
How long does it take? ▾
We process orders in batches. After your batch closes, allow 2-3 weeks for production and shipping. You'll get email updates at every stage — Received, In Progress, Printing, and Shipped.
Can I order just one product? ▾
Yes. You can order any combination — one product, two, or all three. The intake form covers everything in one pass regardless of what you order.
What if something is wrong with my order? ▾
Every order goes through a review before print. If anything looks off — wrong details, a field that didn't carry over correctly — we catch it before it ships. Reply to any of your confirmation emails and we'll fix it.
